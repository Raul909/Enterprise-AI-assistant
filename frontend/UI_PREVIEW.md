## Color Palette

- **Primary Gradient**: #667eea â†’ #764ba2 (Purple/Blue)
- **Background**: White (#ffffff)
- **Surface**: Light Gray (#f7fafc)
- **Text Primary**: Dark Gray (#1a202c)
- **Text Secondary**: Medium Gray (#718096)
- **Border**: Light Gray (#e2e8f0)
- **Error**: Red (#c53030)
- **Success**: Green (#38a169)

## Typography

- **Font Family**: System fonts (-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Segoe UI, Roboto)
- **Headings**: 20-28px, Bold
- **Body**: 14-16px, Regular
- **Small**: 12-13px, Regular

## Spacing

- **Container Padding**: 16-48px
- **Element Gap**: 8-24px
- **Border Radius**: 8-20px
- **Box Shadow**: Subtle elevation

## Animations

- **Fade In**: 0.3s ease
- **Slide Up**: 0.5s ease
- **Hover**: 0.2s transform + shadow
- **Typing Indicator**: 1.4s bounce
